How We Analyze Claims

Claim Amount Analysis

We compare claim amount vs. policy coverage. Very high or maxed-out claims are flagged as suspicious.

Policy Details Verification

We check the time gap between policy start date and claim date. Claims filed shortly after policy purchase are investigated.

Incident Analysis

We examine incident date vs. claim date, location patterns, and type of incident for inconsistencies.

Claimant History Check

We analyze past claim history and demographic information to identify potential fraud patterns.
